    Trail Hotel

    Featuring free WiFi in the rooms, Trail Hotel offers accommodation within 2.2 km from Ikoyi Golf Club. Situated in Victoria Island district, the venue is set 2.9 km from Social Pool.

    The property is located in the popular district of Lagos. You'll be 100 metres from Five Cowries Creek. Lagos Oriental Hotel is a few minutes' drive from Oniru Beach Resort.

    The hotel features non-smoking rooms with climate control, lounge area and sound-proofed windows for guests' use. Some of the en suite units have views of the sea. Certain units are equipped with a fridge, coffee/tea makers and glassware.

    Salma's Authentic Lebanese Cuisine is an a la carte restaurant specialised in Middle Eastern cuisine. The café bar features a TV and a spacious terrace. Relax in an outdoor pool or enjoy a living area, a flat screen TV and a bar.
